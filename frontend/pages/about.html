About Service Sphere

Connecting you with trusted local professionals since 2020

Our Mission

At Service Sphere, we believe that finding reliable local services should be simple, transparent, and stress-free. Our platform bridges the gap between customers and trusted professionals, creating a community built on quality and trust.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Service Sphere started as a small directory for home services and has grown into a comprehensive platform covering over 50+ service categories. We've helped over 100,000 customers connect with verified professionals in their local area.

Why Choose Us

  • Verified Professionals: Every service provider undergoes a thorough verification process.
  • Authentic Reviews: Real feedback from real customers to help you make informed decisions.
  • Fast Connections: Get quotes and responses within hours, not days.
  • Secure Platform: Your data and communications are always protected.
